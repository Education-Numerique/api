Updates
=======

If you'd like to stay up to date on the community and development of LxxL,
there are several options:

GitHub
------

The best way to track the development of LxxL is through
`the GitHub repo <https://github.com/Education-Numerique/api>`_.

Twitter
-------

I often tweet about new features and releases of LxxL.

Follow `@lxxl <https://twitter.com/lxxl>`_ for updates.
